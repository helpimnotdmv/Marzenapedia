How to Edit Marzenapedia

Writing Articles

Articles live in articles/ as .md files. Click the Edit tab on any article, write your content in Markdown, and click Save to GitHub — the page generates a pre-filled commit URL.

When you push to main, a GitHub Action automatically rebuilds index.json, so your new article appears on the homepage and in search instantly.

Saving via GitHub

The "Save to GitHub" button opens a pre-filled commit page on GitHub.com. Confirm the commit there. Your article will be live within seconds, and index.json will be rebuilt automatically by the workflow in .github/workflows/build-index.yml.
